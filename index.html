GMail add-on for Trello

Create cards or comment to existing cards in Trello from GMail

Features

  • Create a new card from an email's subject, message and attachments
  • Append email message as comment optionally
  • Adds a link on Trello card to the original email
  • Remembers previous board and list
  • Supports adding a due date, members and labels to the newly created card
